About YourShop

YourShop is the world's leading classifieds platform, offering local communities in fast-growing markets a vibrant online marketplace. YourShop connects people to buy, sell or trade used items and services in a quick and easy way so anyone can post an ad over the phone or computer. Every month, hundreds of millions of people around the world use YourShop to find and sell a wide range of products, including computers, mobile phones, furniture, sporting goods, services, cars, real estate and more.
